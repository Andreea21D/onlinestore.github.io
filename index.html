About Us

Started in 2000, Food. is the realization of Mr Asif Iqbal's passion for quality food. And he firmly believes that quality need not always have a massive price tag. A fact clearly evident in our pricing! Our regulars usually comprise of college students, corporate employees and tourists.

Nourishing, wholesome and completely irresistible, Food. is on a mission to introduce our uniquely healthful menu to our community of thinkers, do-ers and lovers of life.
